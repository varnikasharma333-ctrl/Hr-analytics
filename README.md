HR Analytics Dashboard Project
📌 Project Overview
Ye project ek Comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard hai jo organization mein Employee Attrition (Turnover) ko analyze karne ke liye banaya gaya hai. Iska main objective ye pata lagana hai ki log company kyun chhod rahe hain aur kin areas mein improvement ki zaroorat hai.

🚀 Key Insights & Functionality
Dashboard mein ye saari cheezein analyze ki gayi hain:

Attrition by Department: R&D department mein sabse zyada attrition (~56%) dekha gaya hai.

Education Field: Medical aur Life Sciences background wale employees mein attrition rate kaafi high hai.

Age-Specific Trends: 26-35 saal ki age bracket wale employees company sabse zyada chhod rahe hain.

Gender Analysis: Male aur Female attrition rate ko alag-alag age groups par track kiya gaya hai.

Job Satisfaction: Matrix visualization se pata chalta hai ki "Sales Representative" roles mein satisfaction score kam hai.

🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
Tool: Power BI

Data Source: HR Employee Dataset (CSV)

Techniques: Data Cleaning (Power Query), DAX Measures, Data Modeling.
